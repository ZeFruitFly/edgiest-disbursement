Rearrange Your Furniture

You can move and resize your furniture to make everything look just right.

1. Click the hammer at the bottom of your room to enter editing mode and rearrange your furniture.

2. Point the mouse at any piece of furniture in your room. Click to select it then click and drag to move it around the floor.

3. Use the up/down handle to move the furniture off the floor. Use the scaling handle in the upper right to resize the furniture.

4. You can always change your mind. Click the Undo icon on the Arranging room palette to undo a position or size change.

5. When you're done rearranging, click the close box on the Arranging room palette to exit editing mode.
