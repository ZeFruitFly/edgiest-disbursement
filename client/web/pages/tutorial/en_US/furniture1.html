Buy Some Furniture

Furniture brings depth and even more personality to a room.

1. Choose Catalog -> Furniture to find something you like.

2. Click to take a closer look at the many pieces of player-made furniture. Click one you like and buy it!

3. To use your new furniture, choose My Stuff -> Furniture and click the the little house to put it in your room.

4. The room will be in editing mode. Check out the next tutorial to see how you can resize and rearrange your new furniture.

5. Click the gray bar on the left side of the screen to quickly return to your Furniture list if you want to add more furniture to your room.
