Change Your Avatar

Let's go shopping!

1. Choose Catalog -> Avatars to see a few of the avatars you can get.

2. Browse through the various avatars. Click one you like and buy it!

3. To wear it, choose My Stuff -> Avatars and click the the little happy face next to your new avatar.

4. Click on your avatar to see if there are fun animations and actions you can try to express your spendid new self!
