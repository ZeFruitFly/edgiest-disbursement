Upload Stuff

Not enticed by anything in the catalog? Want to get really creative? Make your own stuff!

1. "Furniture" can mean almost anything. Let's bring in one of your own pictures to use as a poster on the wall.

2. Choose My Stuff -> Furniture then click Upload to upload a new piece of furniture.

3. Click Browse and pick an image on your computer to upload. Once you've chosen a file, click Upload to upload the image to Whirled.

4. Enter a name for your new piece of furniture at the top of the dialog where it says Item Name. Then click Save and it will show up in your inventory.

5. Click the little house next to your newly created item to put it in your room. Then use the room editing tools (explained in the rearranging tutorial) to put it on the wall in your room.
