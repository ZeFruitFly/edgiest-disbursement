Update Your Profile

Everyone in Whirled has a Profile page to share interests, find friends, show off or just express themselves.

1. Choose Me -> My Profile to see your Whirled Profile page.

2. Whirled builds a lot of your profile for you, like your friends, groups, game ratings and awards. Click Edit to see what you can add yourself.

3. One way to personalize your profile is with a portrait photo. Click Select New next to the default image to change your portrait image.

4. Choose any of your photos or upload a new one by clicking Upload Image.

5. When you're done updating, click Done to make your changes visible.
