Play Games

Whirled has a growing collection of fun games and playing them earns you Flow!

1. Choose Places -> Whirledwide to check out the popular games. They're in a list on the left side of the page.

2. Click on a game to read more about it and click just me! to play a single player game or try Custom Game to start a multiplayer game.

3. Click Join on a table waiting for players to join that game, or click Create Game to start your own game.

4. By playing games, you'll earn Flow which you can use to buy cool Avatars and fancy Furniture and all sorts of other great stuff from the Catalog.
