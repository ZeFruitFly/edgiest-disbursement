Welcome to the First Whirled!

Whirled is a social online world for chat and multi-player games, powered by player-created content. It is Three Rings' next game, the follow-on to Puzzle Pirates and Bang! Howdy. With Whirled we are trying to do something different -- in Whirled the players create the characters, rooms and (in due course) the games. We hope that this will be a lot of fun for everyone!

To get started, you should head into the Whirled. You will need to login with your email address and the password you were sent. Please then click "Me" -> "Account" and configure your Permaname which will allow you to log into the Forums and the Wiki. You can access the Forums and Wiki from the menu navigation in the Whirled once you've logged in.

We will be active on the forums and in the world so feel free to post questions and comments there or say hello if you see us in Whirled.

Thanks again for coming and playing around with our very incomplete new project. We look forward to seeing and playing with the cool stuff you make.
